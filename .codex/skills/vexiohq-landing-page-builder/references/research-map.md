## How To Apply Prior Research

### Messaging

Prefer these conclusions:

- position VexioHQ as founder-led and expert-led
- separate founder experience from company track record
- avoid inflated metrics and unsupported trust claims
- favor direct, specific language over broad AI hype

### UX and conversion

Use earlier research to evaluate:

- hero message direction
- CTA hierarchy
- trust-signal placement
- landing-page structure and supporting sections

Do not blindly implement every earlier suggestion. Cross-check it against the current route tree, current page code, and current credibility guidance.

### SEO and metadata

Use earlier SEO docs to improve:

- metadata quality
- heading structure
- internal linking
- schema coherence
- landing-page keyword targeting

Do not preserve earlier SEO recommendations if they depend on unsupported claims, fake tokens, nonexistent routes, or stale branding.

## Practical Rule

When asked to "look at previous research documents," read the strategy hub, the credibility and solo-positioning docs, and one of the two unified blueprints first. Only go deeper into SEO planning docs when the task specifically needs SEO or metadata direction.
