## Appendix B: Brand Voice Guidelines

### Tone:
- Authoritative but approachable
- Technical but not jargon-heavy
- Confident without arrogance
- Results-focused

### Never Say:
- "Cheap" or "affordable"
- "Small company" or "startup"
- "Try" or "attempt"
- "Hope" or "maybe"

### Always Say:
- "Investment" not "cost"
- "Partnership" not "vendor"
- "Transformation" not "project"
- "Outcomes" not "deliverables"
