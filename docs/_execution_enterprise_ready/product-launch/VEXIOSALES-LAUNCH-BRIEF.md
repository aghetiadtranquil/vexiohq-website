# VexioSales Launch Brief

Status: Execution brief for product-page build.  
Source type: New launch document created from existing research and current repo state.  
Source documents remain unchanged.

## Launch Goal

Ship a credible VexioSales launch page that explains the product clearly, matches current repo evidence, and supports outreach without overstating product readiness.

## Product Status

Recommended status for this page: `Private Beta`

Rationale:

- the repo review flags GTM and capability drift on the current page
- hostname and signup direction are not fully settled
- current copy overstates ready-now scope

## Target User

Primary audience:

- founder-led sales teams
- early RevOps teams
- SMB and mid-market B2B teams evaluating AI-assisted outbound workflows

Secondary audience:

- operators who want better lead qualification, follow-up consistency, and pipeline visibility without adding more manual work

## Core Problem

Most sales teams lose time across four repetitive workflows:

- qualifying inbound and outbound leads
- researching accounts before outreach
- drafting timely follow-ups
- keeping pipeline status current enough to act on it

VexioSales should be presented as a focused operating layer for those workflows, not as a fully autonomous replacement for the revenue team.

## What Is Available Now

Allowed “now” framing:

- AI-assisted lead qualification
- account and contact research support
- response drafting and follow-up suggestions
- pipeline visibility and operational alerts
- conversational query layer for sales data

## What Must Not Be Claimed

- outbound voice calling as a settled launch capability
- fully autonomous selling
- large customer counts
- win-rate or ROI metrics not supported in repo evidence
- production-readiness claims that exceed `Private Beta`
- direct signup as the primary CTA while hostname direction remains unclear

## Metadata Direction

Use practical language:

- AI sales workflow platform
- lead qualification
- research and follow-up
- pipeline visibility
- private beta

Avoid:

- “make calls”
- “close more deals” as a guaranteed outcome
- “now available” unless the launch stage changes
