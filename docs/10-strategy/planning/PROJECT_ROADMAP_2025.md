## Risk Management

### High Risk Items
1. **Build errors blocking deployment**
   - Mitigation: Immediate fixes, better testing
2. **Content delays**
   - Mitigation: Content calendar, multiple writers
3. **Performance degradation**
   - Mitigation: Continuous monitoring, optimization sprints

### Medium Risk Items
1. **Third-party service failures**
   - Mitigation: Fallback systems, multiple providers
2. **SEO ranking drops**
   - Mitigation: Regular audits, quick fixes
3. **Security vulnerabilities**
   - Mitigation: Regular security audits, updates
