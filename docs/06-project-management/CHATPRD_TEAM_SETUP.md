## Step-by-Step Team Setup

### Option 1: Create a New Team (Recommended)

1. **Go to ChatPRD**
   - Visit https://chatprd.ai
   - Log in with your account

2. **Create a Team**
   - Look for "Create Team" or "Teams" in the navigation
   - Click on "Create New Team"
   - Enter team details:
     - Team Name: **VexioHQ**
     - Team URL: vexiohq.chatprd.ai (or similar)

3. **Choose Team Plan**
   - Select a team plan (may have free trial)
   - Team plans typically include:
     - Unlimited integrations
     - Shared workspace
     - Team collaboration features
     - Linear integration access

4. **Invite Team Members**
   - Add team members by email
   - Suggested invites:
     - atul@vexiohq.com (you)
     - Other developers/PMs in your team

### Option 2: Join Existing Team

1. **Check with your organization**
   - Ask if VexioHQ already has a ChatPRD team
   - Request invite from team admin

2. **Accept Team Invitation**
   - Check email for invite
   - Click acceptance link
   - Join the team workspace

## After Creating/Joining Team

### 1. Verify Team Access
- Go to ChatPRD dashboard
- You should see team workspace selector
- Switch to team workspace (not personal)

### 2. Access Team Settings
- Navigate to **Team Settings > Integrations**
- You should now see Linear integration available
- No more "Teams Only" error!

### 3. Connect Linear
- Click "Connect Linear" 
- Authorize with your Linear account
- Select VexioHQ workspace

## Troubleshooting

### Still seeing "Teams Only" error?
1. Ensure you're in team workspace, not personal
2. Check account type in settings
3. Verify billing/subscription status
4. Contact ChatPRD support

### Can't create team?
- May need to upgrade account first
- Check if email domain has existing team
- Contact support@chatprd.ai for help

### Integration not showing?
- Refresh page
- Log out and back in
- Clear browser cache
- Try incognito mode
